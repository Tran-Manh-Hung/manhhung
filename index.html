NATURE TRAVELS LIFE SOME OTHER TOPICS

THE FIELD OF PURPLE TULIPS

Purple Tulips – symbolizing royalty (as purple has long been known as a royal color), this color is often used for brides bouquets on their big day. Purple also symbolizes rebirth, therefore being the perfect color for spring.

THINKING

A practical work inspired by daily life in the series of social distancing days, this time is really precious for us to think about everything, exercise, improve our skills. In the picture is a guy who takes advantage of this time to think about his ideas, creating many other works that bring positive meanings to the community. Stay at home but don't go "Stay at home", so please follow the rules to fight the pandemic together.

GREEN

Green is a work that I made and was inspired by the fact that the natural habitat of wild animals is shrinking significantly. Through "Green" expressing the natural beauty is never boring and should not harm this natural beauty. Why don't we protect the environment as we protect ourselves, to avoid one day we can no longer see those animals in nature even in real life but only know them through books.
